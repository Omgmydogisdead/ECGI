2022 Essay Competition on Global Issues

ECGI is a youth-led essay competition that is welcome to middle and high school students around the world. Being inspired by the United Nations’ 17 Sustainable Development Goals, our aim is to encourage the young generation to voice out their beliefs, values, and prospects regarding current affairs and sustainable development.

Entering our competition enables our participants to develop open-mindedness and global consciousness, as well as critical analysis, logical reasoning, and creative thinking skills. Participation in ECGI will demonstrate the participant’s global citizenship.

Competition Details

What to write | When to write

  • The competition will run until September 15th, 11:59pm BJT. Late entries will not be accepted.
  • The competition is open to students aged 10~18 of any gender from any country.
  • Each participant can submit a maximum of one work for each category. Only individual entries will be accepted.
  • Entries must be original and written in English. Any form of plagiarism will be an academic integrity issue and will result in automatic disqualification.
  • If any sources are used, it should be referenced using a standard referencing system (MLA, APA, Harvard).

Categories
