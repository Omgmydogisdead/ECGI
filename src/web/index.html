Who Are We

ECGI is a youth-led essay competition that is welcome to middle and high school students around the world. Being inspired by the United Nations’ 17 Sustainable Development Goals, our aim is to encourage the young generation to voice out their beliefs, values, and prospects regarding current affairs and sustainable development.

Entering our competition enables our participants to develop open-mindedness and global consciousness, as well as critical analysis, logical reasoning, and creative thinking skills. Participation in ECGI will demonstrate the participant’s global citizenship.
